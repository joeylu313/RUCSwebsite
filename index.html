Rutgers Club Swimming Newsletter

Rutgers Club Swimming provides all Rutgers undergraduate and graduate students, regardless of swimming ability, with an opportunity to continue their swimming careers at a competitive level without the intense time commitment of a varsity sport.

Practices are held 4 days per week (Monday-Thursday from 8:30 PM - 10 PM) at the Sonny Werblin Recreation Center on Busch Campus, and meets are typically held 5 times per semester, which include away meets at other colleges/universities and home meets (Practices and meets are not mandatory).

Swimmers are also provided with social events and fundraising opportunities to get involved outside the pool! Active members are required to pay $30 dues per semester.
